Biography

Currently I am a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Min-Ling Zhang in School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University and a member of PALM group. I received my M.Sc. degree from Southeast University in June 2023 and B.Eng. degree from School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Wuhan University of Technology in June 2020.

My research interests mainly include machine learning and data mining. Currently I am particularly interested in weakly-supervised learning, i.e., how to enable machines to deal with "ambiguity".
