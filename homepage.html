Biography

Currently I am a second-year master student of School of Computer Science and Engineering in Southeast University and a member of PALM group. I received my B.Eng. degree from School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence in Wuhan University of Technology in June 2020. In September 2020, I was admitted to study for a master degree under the supervision of Prof. Min-Ling Zhang in Southeast University without entrance examination.

My research interests mainly include machine learning and data mining. Currently I am particularly interested in weakly-supervised learning, i.e., how to enable machines to deal with "ambiguity".
